Goal:
Implement a bounded necromantic control layer so disguise, transport, host occupation, construct fabrication, and undead command progress as linked operational stages rather than isolated one-off effects.

Scope:

* support staged necromantic capabilities from concealment and movement through possession and command
* support layered possession states distinguishing full override, subtle cohabitation, and brief emergency seizure rather than one binary dominated state
* support opportunistic host-acquisition rules for misery-fed or body-hopping entities rather than assuming they always choose optimal targets
* support dormancy maintenance through worship or adoration so a corpse or avatar remains asleep until ritual maintenance fails and then wakes as a controller-scale threat
* support cult initiation through blood-oath bonds that create remote tracking and punishment channels with ceremony-phase interruption vulnerabilities
* support host body theft routed through fetish-like tools and stolen personal objects, with displaced souls warehoused in bespoke receptacles
* support bargain enforcement that is literal and adversarial, with phrasing itself acting as a trap surface rather than flavor text
* support corpse-host or body-maintenance states where relevant
* support construct-undead fabrication and later command-hierarchy growth
* support high-tier dead entities retaining selected prior-life skills or offices as part of their authority profile
* support non-combat servant functions including remote sensing through undead meshes, delegated standing orders, magical officer cadres, living disciples, and coerced elite subordinates
* support subordinate ascension under bondage terms where the superior retains the subordinate's continuity anchor, preserving both obedience and rebellion pressure inside the same hierarchy
* support telepathic mesh populations in which several servants share obedience and limited awareness through the controlling nexus rather than operating as isolated brutes
* support command-oriented social presence as the primary authority surface for undead or monstrous bodies, replacing ordinary attractiveness with domination bandwidth, force of will, and intimidation hierarchy
* connect the layer to covert organizations, artifact use, and long-term faction operations where relevant

Constraints:

* deterministic only
* no unrestricted necromancy sandbox
* no assumption that necromancy is only encounter-local damage or summon output
* no assumption that servant systems are limited to combat bodies
* no assumption that breaking a control link neutralizes an entity that retains its own motive packet
* prefer compact stage packages over sprawling spell-tree simulation
* keep possession tier, dormancy maintenance state, servant hierarchy, and mesh cohesion legible enough for planning and debugging

Acceptance criteria:

* at least one necromantic path escalates through more than one operational stage
* at least one organization or long-horizon actor can sustain that control layer over time
* at least one possession case distinguishes between full override, subtle cohabitation, and brief emergency seizure
* at least one entity remains dormant through maintenance feeding and wakes as a controller-scale threat when maintenance fails
* at least one servant hierarchy supports non-combat functions such as remote sensing, delegated orders, or logistics roles
* at least one subordinate exists under bondage terms where the superior retains the continuity anchor
* targeted tests or validation examples cover deterministic staged necromantic behavior, possession layering, dormancy maintenance, non-combat servant roles, and mesh obedience

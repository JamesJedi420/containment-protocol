Goal:
Implement a bounded institutional-espionage layer so major courts and strongholds can run internal spies, external spies, and counterspies as part of normal governance rather than as one-off plot twists.

Scope:

* support named offices or staff roles dedicated to internal surveillance, external intelligence gathering, and counterintelligence
* allow local court, subordinate holdings, and outside targets to each carry different espionage reach and exposure surfaces
* support leak detection, loyalty checks, and nested reporting chains inside the same institution
* support ideologically primed intermediaries who become real-world bridges for hostile networks not through overt recruitment but through prior alignment with the system's framing, with modernization projects, scanning workflows, and technical-upgrade culture providing institutional cover and delegitimizing caution until compromise is already established
* support major site failures that begin with a socially manipulated insider enabling reconnaissance, theft attempts, route access, and later full-site displacement through repeated trust breaches rather than direct assault, with compromise remaining distinct from overt domination in that the actor is persuaded, seduced, blackmailed, or ideologically turned without literal mind control
* support trusted public-facing institutions including hospices, libraries, and religious houses that hide predatory or transgressive cells whose outer role grants victim access, evidence control, and moral cover for the inner operation
* support tiered membership structures where inner members understand or deliberately exploit the real agenda while outer actors and staff misread the same institution as benign or routine
* support strategic monitoring anchored into civic infrastructure at dedication time so bridges, gates, public works, and similar structures quietly carry occult surveillance or ward payloads after consecration, remaining hidden behind ordinary civic function until triggered or operationally read
* support clandestine institutions with family continuity, blood ties, apprenticeship, staged recruitment, compartmentalized testing, and engineered cleanup of failed candidates rather than flat interchangeable recruitment
* support performer networks, priestly pipelines, and other legitimate institutions functioning as covert movement meshes, aid networks, or victim-selection surfaces while preserving a mundane public face
* support betrayal or treason sensing at organizational scope that can reveal compromise somewhere in the chain without supplying direct attribution, shifting response into paranoid investigation and loyalty review rather than immediate accusation, so the institution holds unresolved knowledge that treason exists while lacking clean evidence
* distinguish institutional espionage from ordinary faction reputation or ad hoc scouting alone
* connect the layer to legitimacy, court governance, unrest, and intrigue systems where relevant

Constraints:

* deterministic only
* no full intelligence-agency sandbox
* no assumption that all courts use flat public authority with no covert layer
* no assumption that institutional compromise requires overt hostility or literal mind control to be present
* no assumption that trusted public institutions are safe from inner predation when their outer role provides legitimate access
* prefer compact spy-state packets over sprawling clandestine simulation
* keep espionage reach, exposure surface, compromise depth, and treason-detection attribution state legible enough for planning and debugging

Acceptance criteria:

* at least one institution runs distinct internal and external intelligence functions
* at least one counterspy or leak-detection path changes downstream trust or action legality
* at least one subordinate holding participates in the same espionage chain
* at least one insider enables site compromise through persuasion or ideological alignment rather than overt recruitment or mind control
* at least one trusted public institution hides a predatory or transgressive inner operation behind its legitimate outer role
* at least one tiered membership structure preserves uneven knowledge between inner and outer participants
* at least one betrayal-detection path reveals organizational-scope compromise without supplying direct attribution, forcing investigation rather than immediate accusation
* targeted tests cover deterministic surveillance, leak detection, counterintelligence outcomes, ideological compromise, and institution-concealed inner operations
